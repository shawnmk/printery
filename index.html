A Few Tips

For our photos and fine art prints, we typically leave an extra .25" - .5" border around the edges for handling and framing/matting, but it's entirely up to you - we can trim to any size you'd like.

Files differ as to the resolution necessary for printing, typically they need to be at least 180 dpi at the desired print size. But you don't need to worry about that (unless you want to!), just send us the highest resolution files you have and we can take it from there. Some images hold up well at lower resolutions and some require higher resolutions to look good.

If you aren't sure what that means or just want as large of a print as possible from your file, leave it to us and we'll make it happen. If we have any concerns about the quality, we'll let you know before we print. Our job is to make your prints look amazing.
